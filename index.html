Automate your Toll Reimbursements

If you’re unlucky to live in a state with tolls, you know how much labor it takes to get everything reimbursed from your guests. California, New Jersey, and of course Florida are especially bad. Some of the hosts from these states are dealing with upwards of hundred tolls per day!

At CarSync, we always tackle the biggest pain points that P2P hosts experience and so it was a no-brainer to provide a solution for tolls. And so, with CarSync, you can now fully automate your toll reimbursements!

Get everything going in minutes:

  • C onnect your toll agency. We support Sunpass, Florida Epass, Ezpass NJ & NY, Fastrak, TollRoads, GooToGo, Ipass, TxTag, ExpressToll, HCTRA, PeachPass, and Getaround Autotoll (multiple accounts supported).
  • C arSync syncs your toll receipts daily and matches them to cars and trips automatically.
  • N o tolls will be missed. You can be sure that nothing will slip through the cracks. We took great care in designing for reliability. Tolls that don’t match to trips are provided for your inspection.
